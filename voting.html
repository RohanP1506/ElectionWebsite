Register to Vote in Texas

How to Register to Vote in Texas

To register to vote in Texas, you can follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Texas Secretary of State's website at sos.state.tx.us.
  2. Download and complete the Texas voter registration application.
  3. Mail your completed application to your county voter registrar. You can find the address on the application form.

Make sure to register at least 30 days before the election.

Additional Information

For more details, check the Texas voting FAQ.

Early Voting in Texas

Early voting in Texas allows you to cast your ballot before Election Day. Here are the details:

  • Early voting typically begins 17 days before an election and ends 4 days before Election Day.
  • You can vote at any early voting location in your county.
  • Bring an acceptable form of ID, such as a Texas driver's license or a U.S. passport.

Check your local county's election website for specific early voting locations and times.
