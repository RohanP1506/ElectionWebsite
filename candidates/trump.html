Donald Trump: Campaign Ideals for 2024

Explore Donald Trump's vision for America's future.

Core Principles

Rebuild the Greatest Economy in History

Trump aims to restore record-setting tax relief for the middle class, create new jobs, and reduce regulations. His policies previously resulted in higher wages and reduced poverty.

Fair Trade for the American Worker

Committed to fair trade, Trump canceled the Trans-Pacific Partnership, replaced NAFTA with the USMCA, and imposed tariffs on unfair trade practices, prioritizing American jobs.

Unleash Energy Dominance

Trump aims to achieve energy independence by promoting domestic production of oil and gas, while reversing policies from the Harris-Biden Administration that he claims undermine this goal.

Secure Borders and Reclaim National Sovereignty

Trump plans to enhance border security, end catch-and-release policies, and implement a merit-based immigration system to protect American workers and public safety.

War on the Drug Cartels

He intends to combat drug cartels aggressively, implement a naval embargo, and seek maximum penalties for drug traffickers to protect communities from drug-related violence.

Stop Crime and Restore Safety

Trump aims to restore law and order by increasing funding for police, strengthening protections for law enforcement, and addressing rising crime rates in cities.

Renew American Strength and Leadership

Committed to rebuilding the military, Trump seeks to maintain peace through strength and protect America against all threats while avoiding unnecessary foreign conflicts.

Reject Globalism and Embrace Patriotism

Trump advocates for a foreign policy focused on American sovereignty, emphasizing strong international partnerships and rejecting detrimental global agreements.

Care For Our Veterans

Trump prioritizes veteran care through reforms aimed at improving medical services and decreasing veteran homelessness and unemployment.

He supports school choice, opposes critical race theory in education, and aims to empower parents in their children's schooling and educational choices.

Defend Law and Liberty

Trump is committed to appointing judges who uphold constitutional principles and protect the rights of citizens, including the Second Amendment and religious freedoms.

End Censorship and Reclaim Free Speech

He aims to dismantle censorship regimes, ensuring free speech is protected and advocating for transparency in communication from government agencies.

Free, Honest and Lawful Elections

Trump focuses on election integrity by implementing measures to verify voter identity and banning practices that compromise election security.

Drain the Swamp of Washington Corruption

He seeks to eliminate corruption in Washington D.C. through term limits, banning campaign funding, and reforming federal bureaucracies.

Better Health Care Choices at Lower Costs

Trump aims to increase healthcare competition, lower drug prices, and protect Medicare and Social Security, while restoring patient rights and medical freedom.
