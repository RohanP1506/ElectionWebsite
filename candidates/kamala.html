Kamala Harris: Campaign Ideals for 2024

Discover what Kamala Harris stands for and her vision for America.

Core Principles

Opportunity Economy

Kamala Harris believes that a strong middle class is vital for America's strength. As President, she will create an Opportunity Economy that provides every American, regardless of where they live, the chance to compete and succeed.

Tax Cuts for the Middle Class

Harris plans to cut taxes for over 100 million working families by restoring and expanding the Child Tax Credit and Earned Income Tax Credit, ensuring no one earning less than $400,000 pays more in taxes.

Affordable Housing

Committed to making homeownership attainable, Harris will build three million new affordable housing units and provide first-time homebuyers with up to $25,000 for down payments.

Support for Small Businesses

Harris will invest in small businesses and entrepreneurs, aiming for 25 million new business applications and increased access to capital, especially for minority-owned businesses.

Health Care for All

Harris is committed to making healthcare affordable by strengthening the Affordable Care Act, capping insulin prices, and addressing medical debt to ensure healthcare is a right, not a privilege.

Education and Child Care

She will fight for affordable education and childcare, seeking to alleviate student loan debt and provide high-quality educational opportunities for all children.

Climate Action

Harris will tackle the climate crisis by building on the Inflation Reduction Act, creating clean energy jobs, and ensuring environmental justice for all Americans.

Social Security and Medicare

She is dedicated to protecting and strengthening Social Security and Medicare, ensuring that all Americans can rely on these essential benefits.
